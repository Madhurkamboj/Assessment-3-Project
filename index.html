RoomyPlanner

RoomyPlanner seeks to make the roommate lifestyle more convenient, making the conversation of “who’s doing what chore”? or “how much are bills and when are they due?” easier to decide and keep track of when everything is laid out in an application.

Plan your events

A shared calendar for all types of events that need to be completed, whether it be chores to be done, bills to be paid or other occasions.

It can display the events for each week, month or year. The events calendar can also be displayed in a list view, which can be sorted either by date, person assigned, or type of event.

Manage your expenses

A spreadsheet that can log expenses and calculate them against your current savings.

stores information like the name, amount, payment date (if any) and frequency of the expense. A pie chart at the top displays the ratio of each expenses amount, so the user can see which expenses are the largest.
