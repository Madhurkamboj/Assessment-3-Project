RoomyPlanner

RoomyPlanner seeks to make the roommate lifestyle more convenient, making the conversation of “who’s doing what chore”? or “how much are bills and when are they due?” easier to decide and keep track of when everything is laid out in an application.

Expense Management

This feature works similarly to the Chore Allocation feature but is instead for expenses or bills for rent. The user can get in touch with the renter to set up the dates for rent each month, which will be displayed on the calendar.
